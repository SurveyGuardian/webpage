SurveyGuardian



Welcome to SurveyGuardian, the innovative plugin designed for the LimeSurvey platform. At the heart of any research lies the integrity and quality of data collected through online surveys. However, this data can often be compromised by inattentive participants or those engaging in fraudulent activities. SurveyGuardian is our solution to this pressing issue. Developed by a dedicated team of researchers and developers, SurveyGuardian seamlessly integrates with LimeSurvey to enhance the reliability of your data collection process. It employs sophisticated mechanisms to detect and filter out inattentive responses and non-human interactions, ensuring that only genuine and attentive participation contributes to your research findings.

Features


Automatic Attention Checks
SurveyGuardian automatically creates adequate attention checks and provides the necessary tools to remove participants who failed these.

Bot Detection
SurveyGuardian includes the FingerprintJS BotD framework in version 1 (v1) for detecting bots.

User-Friendly Integration
Easy to install and activate, with a backend configuration that is simple and intuitive.

Localization
Language Support: English Spanish German French Russian Mandarin Chinese

Requirements
For using the plugin, LimeSurvey version 5.3.14 or higher needs to be installed on a web server. Therefore the server needs at least 250 MB of disk space, the relational database system MySQL version 5.5.3 or later, and PHP version 7.2.5 or later. To access the LimeSurvey back-end, a screen resolution of at least 1280px * 1024px is needed to manage the plugin. The plugin needs to be installed (in Configuration > Plugins) on the administrator page and then be activated.
